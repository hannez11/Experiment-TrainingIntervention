Welcome to the experiment!
Thank you very much for your participation.
  • This experiment is conducted by the Justus-Liebig-Universität Gießen. Your answers will be treated completely anonymously. The experiment administration cannot draw any conclusions about you as a person.
  • Please read all instructions and tasks carefully. Within the experiment several attention checks are asked. If it becomes apparent that you have often worked inattentively, you will not be compensated.

  • There are no risks associated with participating in this HIT. This is a managerial instead of a psychological experiment. Therefore, you will not be fooled at any time.

In the following three tasks you have to make different decisions.
  • Please note that these three tasks are independent of each other.
  • After completing, you have to justify one of your given decisions to a randomly chosen question out of the three tasks.
  • Therefore, you must be able to list reasons why you decided this way. Please note that the focus should be on the decision-making process and not on its outcome. This means that you have to be able to explain your thoughts on how you arrived at your decisions. Your evaluation will be based on the use of effective decision strategies.
  • After finishing this HIT, some participants will be contacted by e-mail to explain their justification in more detail. In order to guarantee anonymity, the contact is mediated by an independent third person.
